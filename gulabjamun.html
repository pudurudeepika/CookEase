Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • Warm milk as needed
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 tablespoon rose water or saffron (optional)

Procedure

  1. Mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda in a bowl.
  2. Add ghee and mix until the texture is crumbly.
  3. Gradually add warm milk and knead into a smooth dough. Be careful not to overwork the dough.
  4. Shape the dough into small balls, making sure they are smooth and crack-free.
  5. Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan over low to medium heat.
  6. Fry the balls in batches, gently stirring them to ensure even browning. Fry until golden brown.
  7. In a separate pan, make the sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water. Add cardamom powder and rose water or saffron (optional).
  8. Once the sugar syrup is prepared, lower the heat and add the fried gulab jamun balls into the syrup.
  9. Let the gulab jamun soak in the syrup for 30 minutes to an hour before serving.
  10. Serve hot or warm and enjoy!
