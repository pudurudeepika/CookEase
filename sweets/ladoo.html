Laddoo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)
  • Pinch of saffron strands (optional)

Procedure

  1. Heat ghee in a large pan over low-medium heat.
  2. Add the gram flour (besan) and roast it while stirring continuously until it turns golden brown and releases a nutty aroma (10-12 minutes).
  3. Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool slightly.
  4. Mix in powdered sugar, cardamom powder, saffron strands, and chopped nuts until well combined.
  5. While the mixture is still warm, take small portions and shape them into round laddoos using your hands.
  6. Allow the laddoos to cool and set completely before serving.
  7. Store in an airtight container and enjoy your homemade laddoos!
