Bhindi Masala Gravy

Ingredients

  • 250g okra (bhindi), chopped
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon hing (asafoetida)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves (for garnish)

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chili, cook for a minute.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil starts to separate.
  5. Mix in turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Now, add chopped okra (bhindi) and sauté for 4-5 minutes until slightly tender.
  7. Pour in 1/2 cup of water and simmer for 8-10 minutes until the gravy thickens and the bhindi is cooked.
  8. Add garam masala and cook for another 2 minutes.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
  10. Enjoy with roti, paratha, or rice!
