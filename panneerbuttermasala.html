Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 3 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 10-12 cashews (soaked and ground to a paste)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 200-250 grams paneer, cubed
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  4. Stir in tomato puree and cook until oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Add chili powder, turmeric, coriander, and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
  6. Add cashew paste and cook for 2-3 minutes. Pour water and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Add garam masala, kasuri methi, and fresh cream. Stir and cook for 2 more minutes.
  8. Optionally, lightly pan-fry the paneer cubes before adding them to the curry.
  9. Simmer paneer in the curry for 2-3 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  10. Garnish with fresh cream and coriander leaves. Serve hot with naan or rice.
