Jalebi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder (for color)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water (for batter)
  • 2 cups sugar (for syrup)
  • 1/2 cup water (for syrup)
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron strands (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Ghee or oil for frying

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, baking soda, turmeric powder, and yogurt to form a smooth batter.
  2. Gradually add water to make the batter into a thick, flowing consistency. Cover and let it rest for 2 hours.
  3. In a pan, combine sugar, water, saffron strands, and cardamom powder. Heat the syrup until the sugar dissolves completely, then boil for 5-7 minutes until it thickens slightly.
  4. Heat ghee or oil in a pan for frying. To test the temperature, drop a small amount of batter into the oil; it should rise immediately.
  5. Fill a piping bag or squeeze bottle with the batter. Shape the batter into spirals or concentric circles directly into the hot oil, frying until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Remove the fried jalebis and immediately dip them into the hot sugar syrup for about 1-2 minutes. Remove and set aside on a tray to drain excess syrup.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy the crispy and sweet homemade jalebis!
