Dal Tadka

Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. Wash the toor dal thoroughly and pressure cook it with 3 cups of water, turmeric powder, and a pinch of salt until soft and mushy.
  2. In a pan, heat 1 tablespoon of ghee. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add cumin seeds, asafoetida, and dried red chilies. Sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Add chopped onions, green chili, and minced garlic. Cook until onions turn translucent.
  5. Mix in chopped tomatoes and cook until the mixture turns soft and mushy.
  6. Add red chili powder, garam masala, and cooked dal to the pan. Mix well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. In a separate small pan, prepare a tempering by heating 1 tablespoon of ghee. Add garlic slices, dried red chili, and cumin seeds. Pour this over the dal.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with rice or roti.
