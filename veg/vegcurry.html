Vegetable Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 2 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Mix in minced garlic and ginger paste. Cook until aromatic.
  4. Stir in tomato puree and cook until oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin, and coriander powder. Mix well.
  6. Add cubed potatoes, cauliflower, green peas, and carrots. Stir to coat the vegetables with spices.
  7. Pour enough water to cover the vegetables, and let it simmer until vegetables are tender.
  8. Optional: Stir in coconut milk for a richer flavor.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or flatbreads.
