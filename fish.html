Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets (such as tilapia or cod), cut into pieces
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil separates from the masala.
  5. Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Gently add fish pieces into the pan. Stir lightly to coat the fish with the masala.
  7. Pour in coconut milk and water. Stir well and let the curry simmer for 10-15 minutes until the fish is cooked through and the gravy thickens.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and mix gently.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
  10. Enjoy with steamed rice or roti!
