Barfi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup sugar (optional, adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios) for garnish

Procedure

  1. Heat ghee in a non-stick pan over low heat.
  2. Add milk powder and mix well to form a smooth texture.
  3. Pour in condensed milk and stir continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat until the mixture thickens and forms a dough-like consistency.
  5. Add cardamom powder and mix thoroughly.
  6. Remove the mixture from heat and let it cool slightly.
  7. Grease a flat tray or plate with ghee and spread the mixture evenly.
  8. Sprinkle chopped nuts on top and press gently to set them in the mixture.
  9. Let the barfi cool completely, then cut it into square or diamond shapes.
  10. Serve and enjoy this delicious homemade sweet treat!
