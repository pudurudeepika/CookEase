Rasgulla Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-3 cardamom pods (crushed)
  • Rose water or kewra essence (optional)

Procedure

  1. Boil the milk in a deep pan. Once it comes to a boil, lower the heat and add lemon juice or vinegar.
  2. Stir gently until the milk curdles and separates into whey and chenna (paneer).
  3. Strain the chenna using a muslin cloth and rinse it under cold water to remove the sourness of lemon/vinegar.
  4. Hang the chenna for 30 minutes to remove excess water.
  5. Knead the chenna into a smooth dough for about 10 minutes and shape it into small, smooth balls.
  6. In a pressure cooker or a deep pan, boil sugar and water together to make a light syrup. Add the cardamom pods.
  7. Add the chenna balls to the syrup and cover with a lid. Cook on medium heat for 15-20 minutes (pressure cook for 2-3 whistles).
  8. Remove the lid carefully once the pressure releases, or after the balls double in size in the pan.
  9. Allow the rasgullas to cool in the syrup. Add rose water or kewra essence for extra flavor if desired.
  10. Refrigerate and serve chilled.
