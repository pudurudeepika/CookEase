Baingan Bharta

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant (baingan)
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 3 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. Roast the eggplant directly on a gas flame until the skin is charred and the flesh becomes soft.
  2. Once cooled, peel off the skin, and mash the roasted eggplant. Set it aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Mix in ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  7. Add the mashed eggplant, mix well, and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Stir in garam masala and adjust salt as needed.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with roti, paratha, or naan.
