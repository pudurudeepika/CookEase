Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves (for garnish)

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chili, cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates from the masala.
  5. Mix in turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add chicken pieces and sauté for 5-7 minutes, until the chicken is lightly browned.
  7. Pour in water, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for another 2 minutes.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
  10. Enjoy with rice, roti, or naan!
